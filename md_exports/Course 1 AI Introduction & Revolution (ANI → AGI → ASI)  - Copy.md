## Slide 16

What is Intelligence ?

Dimension

Human Intelligence (Gold Standard)

Artificial Intelligence (Today)

Learning

Learns from very few examples (one-shot)

Requires massive labeled datasets

Reasoning

Abstract, analogical, causal

Statistical, correlational

Pattern Recognition

Understands meaning, emotion, social cues

Matches narrow patterns extremely well

Creativity

Generates novel ideas and solutions

Optimizes within predefined rules

Adaptation

Transfers knowledge across domains

Task-specific, poor transfer

Common Sense

Intuitive understanding of the world

Frequently fails at simple reasoning

Context

Meaning changes with situation

Fragile to small input changes

Emotional Intelligence

Empathy, social awareness

None

Self-Awareness

Reflects on its own thinking

No awareness of itself or limits
